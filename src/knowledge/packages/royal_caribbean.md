# Royal Caribbean International — Cruise Fares, Inclusions, Drink & Internet Packages

## Cruise Fare Types

### Standard Rate
- Base cruise fare with the ability to select a specific cabin category.
- Price may fluctuate depending on demand and availability.

### Non-Refundable Deposit Rate
- Lower price compared to the Standard Rate.
- Deposit is non-refundable after booking.
- Changing cruise dates may result in loss of deposit or additional fees.
- In some cases, this fare may include onboard credit as compensation for stricter conditions.

### Flash / Promotional Rates
- Limited-time discounted offers.
- Often do not include additional bonuses or perks.

## Royal Caribbean Promotions
Royal Caribbean regularly offers promotions such as:
- **Kids Sail Free** — children cruise for free when traveling with adults (conditions apply).
- **BOGO 60% Off** — second guest receives up to 60% discount.
- **Early Booking Bonus** — lower fares or bonuses for early reservations.
- **Holiday Deals, Black Friday, Wave Season** — periods with the best discounts.

## What Is Included in the Cruise Fare

### Accommodation
- Stay in the selected cabin category booked with Royal Caribbean International.

### Dining
- Complimentary dining in main restaurants and free cafés for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

### Complimentary Beverages (in dining venues)
- Drinking water
- Coffee
- Tea
- Milk

- Iced tea

### Kids Club

- Pools, sports courts, fitness center, and public areas.

## Not Included in the Cruise Fare
- Gratuities (service charges) — automatically added onboard (approximately $18.50 per person per day).
- Bottled water, specialty coffee, alcoholic beverages (unless a drink package is purchased).
- Shore excursions.
- Wi-Fi.
- Spa, beauty salon, casino.
- Specialty dining restaurants.

## Drink Packages — General Rules
- Drink packages must be purchased for **all guests in the same cabin**.
- Packages are available **only for the full duration of the cruise**.
- Alcohol packages are available only for guests **18+** (or according to local laws of the itinerary).
- Packages can be used in all bars, restaurants, lounges onboard and on CocoCay (for eligible drinks).
- Packages **cannot be used**:
  - in cabin minibars;
  - for room service;
  - in venues where the drink is not listed in the package.
- Drink packages are **personal and non-transferable**.
- Guests may order **only one drink at a time**.
- Some themed bars may offer a limited drink selection.
- Packages do **not include Starbucks® beverages**.
- Alcohol sales may be restricted in certain ports or countries.
- Drink packages are refundable only according to cancellation policies **before embarkation**.
- Once onboard, packages cannot be canceled or modified.
- Royal Caribbean reserves the right to suspend drink packages at any time.
