# Celebrity Cruises — Cruise Fares, Drink Packages, Internet & Gratuities

## Cruise-Only Rate

### Includes
- Accommodation in the selected cabin category (excluding The Retreat and AquaClass).
- Full dining in the main restaurant and Oceanview Café buffet.
- Access to entertainment programs, shows, live music, and onboard parties.
- Access to pools, whirlpools, fitness center, and main ship infrastructure.

### Not Included
- Drinks (available via drink packages or pay-as-you-go).
- Wi-Fi.
- Daily gratuities.
- Service packages or specialty restaurants.

---

## All Included Fare

### Includes
- Everything from Cruise-Only Rate, plus:
- **Classic Drinks Package**:
  - Bottled water
  - Juices
  - Espresso and latte
  - Smoothies
  - Beer, wine, cocktails (up to $10 per drink)
- **Basic Wi-Fi**:
  - Social media, messaging apps, email
  - Streaming and video calls not supported

### Not Included
- Gratuities.
- Premium alcoholic beverages (upgrade available).
- Premium Wi-Fi or streaming.
- Specialty dining restaurants.

## Drink Packages — General Rules
- Packages apply to all adults in the cabin.
- Available for the full cruise only.
- Alcohol packages available for guests 21+ (or per local laws).
- Valid in bars, restaurants, lounges, and Perfect Day at CocoCay (select itineraries).
- Not valid for room service, minibar, or special tastings.
- One drink per order.
- Celebrity Cruises may modify package availability at any time.

---
