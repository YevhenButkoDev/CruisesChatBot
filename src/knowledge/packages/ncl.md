# Norwegian Cruise Line (NCL) — Cruise Inclusions, Free at Sea, Packages & Gratuities

## What Is Included in the Base Cruise Fare

### For All Cabin Categories
- Accommodation in the selected cabin category for the entire cruise.
- Cabin amenities: TV, phone, shower or bathtub, hairdryer, air conditioning.
- Freestyle Dining (flexible dining times, no fixed schedule).
- Complimentary dining venues.
- 24-hour room service (delivery fee applies from 00:00 to 05:00).
- Tea and coffee-making facilities in the cabin.
- Coffee, tea, water, and iced tea during breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
- Onboard entertainment (shows, nightclubs, live music).
- Sports activities (table tennis, basketball, tennis, golf).
- Kids Clubs.
- Fitness center, sports courts, jogging track, pools, and jacuzzis.
- Port taxes, fees, and government charges.

- Luggage unpacking on embarkation day.

- Champagne, bottled water, and fruit on embarkation day.

## Not Included
- Gratuities (service charges, charged onboard).
- Casino, telephone, internet, video games.
- Alcoholic and some non-alcoholic beverages.
- Specialty dining reservations.
- Entry visas (if required).
- Medical insurance.
- Trip cancellation insurance (optional).
- Airfare and transfers.
- Shore excursions.
- Cabin minibar.
- Spa, beauty salon, laundry, and personal services.

## Free at Sea Program
Allows guests to select additional service packages for an extra charge.

### Premium Beverage Package
Includes:
- Unlimited drinks up to $15 per serving.
- Premium cocktails, spirits (e.g., Patron Silver, Grey Goose).
- Wines and beers by the glass.
- 20% discount on champagne and wine bottles.
- Wide selection of non-alcoholic drinks and juices.

Applies to guests 1 and 2 (21+) in the reservation.

---

### Specialty Dining Package
- 2–4 night cruise: 1 dining experience
- 5–6 night cruise: 2 dining experiences
- 7–8 night cruise: 3 dining experiences
- 9+ night cruise: 4 dining experiences

Includes appetizer, main course, and dessert per person.
Additional items charged à la carte.

Applies to guests 1 and 2 in the cabin.

---

### Shore Excursion Credit
- $50 onboard credit for shore excursions.
- Applies to guest 1 only.

---

### Internet Package (Free at Sea)
- 150 minutes total per cruise.
- Applies to guests 1 and 2 only.
- Wi-Fi must be turned off when not in use to avoid consuming minutes.

---

## Wi-Fi Packages

### Voyage Wi-Fi Pass — $29.99 per day per device
- Web browsing, email, messaging.
- No streaming support.

### Streaming Voyage Wi-Fi Pass — $39.99 per day per device
- Streaming services.
- VPN support.

---

## Gratuities
- Studio, Interior, Ocean View, Balcony, Club Balcony Suites: $20 per guest per day (ages 3+).
- The Haven Suites: $25 per guest per day (ages 3+).
