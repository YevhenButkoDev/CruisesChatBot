# Costa Cruises — Fares, Drink Package, Internet Packages, and Gratuities

## My Cruise Fare
This fare is a basic and cost-effective option for guests who want to enjoy a cruise without additional services.

Key features:
- Price: the most affordable option with all essential amenities for comfortable accommodation.
- Cabin selection: ability to choose a specific cabin and its location onboard.
- Dining time selection: guests can choose a convenient dinner seating/time.
- CostaClub points: ability to earn CostaClub loyalty points even before the cruise starts.
- Cancellation policy: flexible booking cancellation conditions.

## All-Inclusive Fare
This fare is suitable for guests who prefer an “all-inclusive” concept and want additional privileges.

Key features:
- Includes the My Drinks beverage package.
- Cabin and dining time selection: ability to choose cabin category, its location, and dinner seating/time.
- CostaClub points: earn loyalty points for future travel.
- Cancellation policy: flexible booking cancellation conditions.

## My Drinks Package — Classic All-Inclusive Choice
The My Drinks Package is the most popular beverage package from Costa Cruises. It includes unlimited drinks available in bars, restaurants, and buffets onboard.

### What is included
- Unlimited soft drinks, fruit juices, and hot beverages (coffee, tea, hot chocolate).
- Various beers and table wines (red, white, rosé, sparkling) served by the glass.
- Classic cocktails, mixed drinks, and spirits (excluding premium brands).
- A selection of non-alcoholic cocktails.
- Drinks are available in all bars, restaurants, and buffets onboard.

### What is not included
- Premium and aged alcoholic beverages, champagne, and premium wines.
- Drinks from the in-cabin minibar.
- Beverage service via room service.

## Last Minute Fare
This fare is intended for guests who are ready to cruise soon and want to take advantage of special offers.

Key conditions:
- Payment: the booking must be fully paid within 24 hours after confirmation.
- Cancellation: cancellation fee is 100%.
- Passenger changes: name/surname changes are not allowed.
Note: This is a promotional fare and may not apply to all cruises.

## Internet Packages (Available for an Extra Charge)
Internet packages onboard Costa Cruises are provided for the entire cruise and cannot be purchased for individual days.

### FULL Internet Package (Unlimited)
- 1 device: €17 per day
- 2 devices: €30 per day

### Social Internet Package
Includes access to social networks/apps:
WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, WeChat, Twitter, LinkedIn, Threema, Pinterest, Tumblr, TikTok, Snapchat, Kakao, Telegram.

- 1 device: €10 per day
- 2 devices: €15 per day

## Service Charge (Gratuities)
Service charges (gratuities) are paid onboard:
- €11 per night / adult
- €5.5 per night / child over 4 years old
